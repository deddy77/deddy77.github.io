Latest Project

Starship

Explore the far reaches of space with “Starship”, an interactive 3D game, developed with Python and Kivy. Board a triangle-shaped spaceship as you navigate randomly generated and winding paths through a stunning cosmic landscape and pleasant background music. Your mission ? Avoid falling into the abyssal void by skillfully maneuvering the ship using the keyboard keys (win/iOS version). Whether you're on mobile, Windows or iOS, your ship is ready to take you on a captivating space adventure where responsiveness and precision are essential to survival. Prepare for an immersive experience that pushes the boundaries of interstellar exploration
